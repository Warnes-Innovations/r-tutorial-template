# Paper-Ball Toss Activity (10-12 min)

## Setup
- Tape 9 letter-size sheets in a `3 x 3` grid on a wall.
- Scoring grid (top to bottom):

| 2 | 4 | 2 |
|---|---|---|
| 4 | 8 | 4 |
| 2 | 4 | 2 |

- Mark one throw line at a fixed distance for everyone.

## Rules
- Each person gets `2` practice throws, then `9` recorded throws in `3` sets of `3`.
- Throw from behind the line with one hand.
- If a throw touches multiple sheets, score the higher value.
- Missed target = score `0`.

## Record Per Throw
- `participant_id`, `set_num`, `toss_in_set`, `throw_num`, `target_cell`, `zone_score`, `hit_flag`, `distance_from_center_cm`.

## Goal
- Build a small real dataset for import, cleaning, summaries, and plots in R.
